### üåü The Challenge Overview
This challenge is a structured program arranged by IDC to dedicate consistent effort towards mastering SQL fundamentals and advanced topics. The goal is to spend at least one hour every day for 21 consecutive days on learning, practicing, and building projects using SQL.

### About Data Set:
Hospital Beds Management - Available at [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jaderz/hospital-beds-management)
There are total 4 tables.

1. patients         ‚Äì patient details, age, service, satisfaction, etc.
2. services_weekly  ‚Äì weekly stats of patients admitted, refused, or requested by service type.
3. staff            ‚Äì staff member details.
4. staff_schedule   ‚Äì work schedule and service allocation details.
